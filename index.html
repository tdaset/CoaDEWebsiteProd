Game Features

Campaign and Sandbox Modes

Assume the role of an admiral and fight through a storyline chronicling the descent of the solar system into war. Or simply play in the sandbox, designing ships and pitting them against one another.

Real Science, Real Technology

Every technology, from the Nuclear Thermal Rockets to the Magnetoplasmadynamic Thrusters to the Railguns, was implemented using actual equations from Engineering and Physics textbooks and white papers.

Extremely Accurate Orbital Mechanics

With an N-Body Simulator (the kind NASA uses to plot actual spacecraft trajectories), all orbital phenomenon from hyperbolic orbits, Lagrange points, and orbital perturbation are all correctly simulated.

All Features

Space Combat Simulator
100% Scientific Realism

For those who want to know what space warfare would actually be like.

No other game combines the extremely accurate orbital mechanics, 1:1 scale of the solar system, and technology which is implemented 100% by scientific equations.

Children of a Dead Earth is a simulation of true-to-life space warfare. Design your spacecrafts using real world technologies. Traverse the solar system using actual orbit mechanics. Command fleets as the solar system descends into war, and see if you have what it takes to become the victor.
