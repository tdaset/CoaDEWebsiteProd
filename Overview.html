What would space warfare actually be like?


The answer is Children of a Dead Earth.

Most science fiction tries to say what space warfare would be like. Children of a Dead Earth is different.

Children of a Dead Earth was created to discover what space warfare would be like. This was done by implementing the equations first, and then extrapolating the gameplay from the emergent behaviors that resulted.

Children of a Dead Earth is a space warfare simulator that is unparalleled in scientific realism.

No other game combines the extremely accurate orbital mechanics, 1:1 scale of the solar system, and technology which is implemented 100% by scientific equations.[Game Features]
